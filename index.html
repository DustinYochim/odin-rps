Instructions

The rules are simple. You will be playing against the computer. You will each choose either rock, paper, or scissors. The winner is determined by the following rules:

  • Rock beats scissors
  • Scissors beats paper
  • Paper beats rock

If you and the computer choose the same thing, it's a tie. You will play five rounds. The player with the most wins at the end of five rounds is the winner.
